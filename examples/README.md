# Example 1
```./dualgrid-generate-linux testing-1```

Input:
- `testing-1`: a 1x5 tileset

Output: 
- `testing-1-15`: a tileset consisting of 15 tiles

# Example 2: alternative tiles
```./dualgrid-generate-linux --alts 1 testing-2```

Input:
- `testing-1`: a 2x5 tileset, or a 1x5 tileset with alternative tiles

Output: 
- `testing-2-15`: a tileset consisting of 30 tiles, or two otputs stitched horizontally
